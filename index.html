About Me

Hello! My name is Rachel Folse. I was born on June 15th, 2001. My favorite color is Pink. My favorite foods are Mac and Cheese and Pizza. For fun, I have several hobbies. I like to weightlift and do yoga up to five times a week. I also like to listen to music. I like all genres including pop, rap, r&b, and rock. Same with the television shows I watch, I like everything from reality shows to anime. I travel every so often. Some of the places I've been are Washington DC, Disney World, Michigan, and North Carolina. I usually journal all these activities in my planner. As far as my professional life goes, I went to Southeastern Louisiana University where I got my undergraduate degree in Strategic Communications. While I was there, I worked at the Lion's Roar Newspaper where I wrote many stories, interviewed students through email, and took photographs using my Canon camera. After I graduated, I got a full time position as a Technology Operations Associate at Wells Fargo. I also am in the Web Design and Online Communication program at the University of Florida.
